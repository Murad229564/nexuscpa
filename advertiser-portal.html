🔗 Postback / S2S Integration
When a publisher drives a conversion, our system fires a GET request to your postback URL with the click ID and payout. Your server must respond with HTTP 200.
Your Postback URL (send to our team)
https://yourserver.com/conversion?click_id={clickid}&payout={payout}&status=approved
Our Conversion Postback (fires to you)
https://track.nexuscpa.com/postback?click_id=XXXX&payout=2.50&token=nexus_secret_2026

Parameters:
{clickid} — Unique click ID from NexusCPA
{payout} — Conversion payout in USD
{aff_id} — Publisher ID
{sub1} — Publisher's sub ID / campaign
